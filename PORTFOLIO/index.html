JKUAT Car Booking Platform System

Developed the JKUAT Car Booking Platform System, a comprehensive web application designed to streamline car reservation and booking processes within the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) campus. The system enables students and staff to easily book campus vehicles for various purposes, including academic and administrative activities.

Key Features:

  • User-friendly interface for car booking requests.
  • Real-time availability status of campus vehicles.
  • Administrative dashboard for vehicle management and scheduling.
  • Integration with JKUAT's user authentication system for secure access.

This project involved extensive collaboration and problem-solving, leading to a successful implementation that significantly improved car booking efficiency at JKUAT.
